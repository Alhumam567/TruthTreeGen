Structural Recursion Formula for well-formed formulae:

    1. Any literal is a formula (e.g.):
        P, Q, Fa, Gbc
    2. Negation of a literal is a formula (e.g.):
        ~P, ~Q, ~Fa, ~Gbc
    2. Any two formulas φ,ψ can be combined using a truth-functor as a new formula with surrounding smooth brackets (e.g.):
        (φ^ψ), (φVψ), (φ->ψ), (φ<->ψ)
    3. Otherwise, it is not a formula
